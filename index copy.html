Shared Multiple Sculpture

Mixed Media, 2017

This object was created as part of an event where each artist participating brought 24 multiples and traded them until they left with one item from each artist and one of their own remaining. This particular piece was composed of folded sticky tape, thread, and wire.

Layered Paint and Pages

Acrylic & Fabric Paint, Printed Photos, Animated Gif, 2017

A small Canvas was painted with a different painting and photographed each day. The photographs were printed and bound into a book, the canvas was sliced to reveal the many layers, and the photos additionally made into an animated image for ease of digital viewing.
